Clinical Run

Imara Performance PT

Running performance, gait analysis, and pelvic health. Founded by Danna Herrick — 3x Olympic Trials marathoner and Ironman World Championships finisher. Clinical gait analysis: 60 min, BCBS covered or $150 cash pay. Requires initial eval. This is not the same as a shoe fitting — it's a plan of care.

norwalkchamber.org →
